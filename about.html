About Me

Background

Born on april 14, 2007, I graduated senior high at ISUDD and went to try out pharmacy. I got into SLU at baguio to study pharmacy but due to complications I left. So I decided to go back to my roots and enrolled back to UDD to study something I'm passionate about, computers. As a child I was always passionate about computers. But I'm mainly more so on the hardware side, as I love building and even repairing different electronics like Laptops, PC, and even mobile phones Now that I'm here in Universidad De Dagupan, I hope to raise my knowledge on coding so I can end up working for cyber security.

Story

There is a reason on why I chose cybersecurity, it was actually my first choice for most of my life. Being exposed to the internet at a young age made me understand the importance of cybersecurity. I've had quite a long history of dealing with hackers, or bad actors on the internet. And I've always had a deep passion on wanting to prevent such situations from happening again. So choosing cybersecurity was more like an obligation and a fulfillment to the promise I made to my younger self. I hope one day I am able to make the internet a much safer place.
